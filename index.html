About me

I'm a Gradute from the Department of Electronic and Telecommunication Engineering, University of Moratuwa, Sri lanka. I am currently working as a Machine Learning Engineer and a Project Manager at Fcode Labs (PVT) LTD. My research interests include Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Algorithm Development and Optimization. I am also broadly interested in Cybersecurity with particular emphasis on privacy and security aspects of machine learning models.
Previously, I worked as a research intern at Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organization's (CSIRO) DATA61 Information Security and Privacy Research Group in 2018. I was supervised by Dr. Suranga Seneviratne and Dr. Guillaume Jourjon. After the completion of my Bachelor's degree, I worked as a Graduate Research Assistant with Dr. Ranga Rodrigo. Currently, I am seeking Ph.D. oppotunities in the field of Machine learning and Cybersecurity.
